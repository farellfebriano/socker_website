About us

At Aubrey Soccer Academy, we are passionate about developing our players and helping them achieve their goals. We provide personalized training programs to each player. We believe that every player has the potential to reach their goals—whether that’s playing at a professional level or earning a scholarship to their dream college.

We believe consistency is key to improvement. Our program is designed around a 3-month training cycle, with 3 sessions per week. This consistent approach helps players build skills, fitness, and mental toughness over time. After each 3-month period, we offer a short break to recover, reflect, and recharge before diving back into another focused cycle.

Why Aubrey Soccer Academy?

Personalized Coaching

We focus on each player individually, offering tailored training and feedback to help them improve and reach their full potential. We are dedicated to helping our commited players to grow and improve, recognizing the time and hard work they invest.

Progress Tracking and Development

We believe that hardwork and consistency pays off over time, and we are commited to showing players and parents their progress. We track each player;s growth closely with monthly assessments in areas like speed, stamina, strength and technical skills.

Individual Goal Settings & Support

We work closely with our players and their families to set clear, personalized goals & training, borth short-term and long-term. Our coaches offer continuous support and guidance to ensure our players stay on track and remain motivated in achieving their dreams.

Valuable Investment

We are commited to each player's growth, promising genuine care and dedication to their development. Our rates are more affordble than other programs as our focus is not about money, more to helping players reach the fullest potential and achieve their dreams.
